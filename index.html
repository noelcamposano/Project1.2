About Us

National Bureau of Investigation (NBI)
Our Mission: At the National Bureau of Investigation, our mission is to uphold the rule of law,
protect the rights of our citizens, and ensure the safety and security of our nation.
We are committed to conducting thorough and impartial investigations, promoting justice, and maintaining the highest standards of professionalism and integrity.
Our History: The National Bureau of Investigation traces its roots back to 1936,
when it was established by Manuel L. Quezon and Jose Yulo. Over the years, we have evolved and expanded
our capabilities to meet the changing needs of our society.
Today, we stand as a symbol of trust and excellence in law enforcement, with a rich history of serving our country with dedication and honor. Our Achievements: Over the years, the NBI has achieved numerous milestones in the field of law enforcement.
We have successfully solved high-profile cases, disrupted criminal networks,
and contributed to a safer and more just society. Our commitment to excellence and our unwavering dedication to our mission drive us to achieve even greater milestones in the future.
